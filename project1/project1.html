How do Search Engines Work?

Search engines are sort of like a dictionary with endless amounts of information. Engines use algorithms from a search index and computers are used to look up this information through these search engines. When a user searches for a particular item or phrase, the search engine will try and provide that user with the best and most relevant results.

Importance of Keywords and Descriptions as Meta Tags

When building a website, keywords and meta tags should be used throughout the site to ensure that your content is relevant to what the user might be looking for. Search Engines will use keywords from your site to gather information about your product and service and showcase it to the user who might be looking for something similar.

Good Hierarchy

When used properly heading tags allow browsers to understand how the page is formatted and what topics are being talked about. For example, H1 heading could have WELCOME TO OUR SHOP on the landing page. A H2 could include a longer more informational heading about a particular product or service and then have a p tag that goes into detail about the product or service.
